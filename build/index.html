The Rise of Cerus

As the first god of the original constellation, Cerus fought to destroy all new constellations emerging in his path in a jealous attempt to be the most powerful in the universe.

No one could stop him, Not one of the thousands of Black Holes, Not the hottest plasma from the sun. He has been proving to be truly unstoppable.

One day in a foreign Universe Cerus was wreaking havoc, Capturing his enemies' most desired treasure. He took immense grasp over what Mortals would call the cerus NFT, designed in his image. Then, in a blink of an eye returned home. The only way to free the mortal's treasure is to take back from his constellation, which means taking back Cerus NFT and in return owning a part of Cerus.

A collection of 500 Unique NFTs where the community takes back whats rightfully theirs. Every seven days when light exceeds its mortal speed limit an owner is gifted what Cerus is hiding in his treasure trove.
